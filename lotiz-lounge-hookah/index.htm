LOTIZ LOUNGE & HOOKAH

Our story

DISCOVER US

Lotiz Lounge & Hookah would be a great choice to relax while watching the view of Marmara Sea, Old City by day or night. Enjoy the splendid view of the city while spending time at the roof.

All elements are developed with the experience and knowledge of Loti Group’s professional team. While all of these elements are critical
for a memorable restaurant experience and the highest level of guest satisfaction, these elements have the same level of importance and coexist in harmony.

Lotiz Lounge & Hookah is the best place to relax and enjoy the fascinating sights of both Istanbul and the Old City while having your eating after a long and tiring day in this city.

You can also have your lunch and dinner at our restaurant during the whole year, or you can have fun with music, cocktails and luxury hookah over there.

Our recently renovated terrace provides you special moments as well during the day and night with city’s outstanding atmosphere.

Take in the breath-taking views of the city from the rooftop terrace and appreciate Istanbul from a whole new perspective.

Offering a modern, elegant and stylish ambience along with a stunning city view, Lotiz Lounge & Hookah is one of the classic dining destinations of Istanbul.  With cuisine showcasing the best of modern European cuisine together with expertly prepared fresh sushi, Lotiz Lounge offers you the ultimate dining experiences in a unique city that unites continents.

With its panoramic view of the Marmara Sea and its cozy atmosphere, Lotiz Lounge is the place to dine in style and embrace a truly unforgettable experience.
